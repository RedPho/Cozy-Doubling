Account Deletion

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how you can delete your Cozy Doubling account and what happens to your data when you do. This process is managed by the developer, grepho.

⚠️
Action is permanent. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered. All focus sessions, statistics, and earned items will be lost.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: In the App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly within the Cozy Doubling app:

1
Open the Settings page from the bottom navigation bar.
2
Scroll down to the Danger Zone section.
3
Tap on Delete Account.
4
Confirm your choice. Your data will be deleted immediately.

Option 2: Via Email

If you no longer have the app installed, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

  • Email: cozydoublingdev@gmail.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Information required: Please send the request from the email address associated with your Google account used in the app, or provide your unique Player Tag.

Email requests are typically processed within 7 business days.

Request Partial Data Deletion

If you would like to keep your Cozy Doubling account but want us to delete specific pieces of data (without deleting your entire account), you can make a request via email.

Examples of Partial Requests:
  • Deleting specific focus session records
  • Clearing your focus statistics
  • Resetting your Friends list

To make a request, email us at cozydoublingdev@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request". Please specify exactly which data you would like removed.

What Data is Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data types are permanently removed from our servers:

Profile Information Display name, Player Tag, and your internal User ID.
Activity History All focus session records, task text, and focus statistics.
Social Data Your friends list and block list.
In-App Economy Your leaves balance and all unlocked themes/cosmetics.

What Data is Kept?

We do not retain any personal data after account deletion. However, please note:

  • Subscription Records: Financial transaction records (for "Supporter" subscriptions) are maintained by Google Play and RevenueCat for accounting and legal purposes. These records are anonymized and no longer linked to your deleted Cozy Doubling profile.
  • Backups: Residual data may remain in our encrypted database backups for up to 30 days before being completely overwritten.
