1 Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information (from Google Sign-In)

When you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account ID and display name from Google. We do not store your email address in our own database.

1.2 Profile Information You Set

  • Display Name — the name shown to your friends and in Focus Rooms. You can change this at any time in Settings.
  • Player Tag — a randomly generated unique tag (e.g., #XA4K6E9F) so friends can find you without sharing personal details.

1.3 Focus & Activity Data

  • Focus Session Records — start time, end time, number of tasks completed, and task text you enter during a session.
  • Statistics — total minutes focused, computed from your sessions.
  • In-App Currency (Leaves) — leaves earned through focus sessions.
  • Equipped Theme — which visual theme you have selected.

1.4 Social Data

  • Friends List — user IDs of friends you have added and the relationship status (pending / accepted).
  • Block List — user IDs of users you have blocked, preventing them from interacting with you.
  • Real-Time Presence — while you are in a Focus Room, your display name, current task text, and task progress are broadcast live to other room participants via Supabase Realtime. Live broadcast data is not permanently stored. However, the last task text and progress from each session is saved to your focus session record so that your friends can see your recent activity.

1.5 Purchase & Subscription Data

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions ("Supporter") purchased through Google Play. We do not process or store your payment card information. We store only your subscription status (active/inactive).

1.6 Technical Data

The App requires internet access to function. Standard network data (such as IP addresses) may be processed by our backend provider (Supabase) in the normal course of service operation.

3 How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Other Users (Limited)

  • Your Display Name and Player Tag are visible to others so they can add you as a friend.
  • When you are in a Focus Room, your Display Name, task text, and task progress are visible to other room participants in real-time.

3.2 With Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following services to operate the App:

Supabase Backend database and authentication. Stores your profile, focus sessions, and friends list. Privacy Policy ↗
Google Sign-In / Google Identity Services Authenticates you with your Google account. Google Privacy Policy ↗
RevenueCat Manages in-app subscriptions and purchase validation. Privacy Policy ↗
Google Play Processes in-app purchase payments. Google Privacy Policy ↗

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests from public authorities (e.g., a court order).

4 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts — We retain your data for as long as your account is active.
  • Focus Session Records — Retained to compute your statistics and populate your journey history.
  • Focus Session Records & Task Text — Retained to compute your statistics and populate your journey history. The last task text from each session is also stored so your friends can see your recent activity. Live broadcast data (streamed during a session) is not separately persisted.
⚠️
Account Deletion is permanent. When you delete your account via Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account, all your data — profile, focus sessions, statistics, friends list, leaves, and themes — is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our servers. This cannot be undone.

5 Data Security

  • All communication between the App and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS / TLS.
  • Authentication uses Supabase Auth with industry-standard JWT tokens.
  • Access to your data is enforced by Row-Level Security (RLS) policies on our database — you can only access your own data.
  • In-app purchases are validated server-side through RevenueCat.

No method of electronic transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

6 Children's Privacy

Cozy Doubling is not directed at children under 13 years of age (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at cozydoublingdev@gmail.com and we will take steps to delete the information promptly.
